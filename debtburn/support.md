# DebtBurn Support

Hit a bug, got a question, or got a feature idea? Drop us a line — every email gets read.

We aim to reply within **5 business days**.

## Before you email, please include

- Your device (e.g. iPhone 15 Pro, Samsung Galaxy S23)
- Your operating system version (Settings → General → About on iOS, Settings → About phone on Android)
- The DebtBurn version (Settings screen, scroll to the bottom)
- A short description of what you were doing when the issue happened
- Screenshots if you have them — they help a lot

## Common topics

### "I lost my data"
DebtBurn stores everything locally on your device. We have no copy on our servers. If you uninstall the app or wipe your phone, the data is gone. Use **Settings → Export your data** regularly to back up.

### "How do I cancel my subscription?"
There's no subscription. DebtBurn is free, with no recurring charges. Pro features are unlocked for everyone.

### "I want to delete my data"
Open the app → Settings → tap each debt's delete button. To remove everything in one go, uninstall the app — that wipes the local database. We never had a copy.

### "Will my data sync between devices?"
Not yet. DebtBurn is local-only by design. Cross-device sync may come in a future update with end-to-end encryption.

### "Why is the debt-free date different from my bank's calculator?"
DebtBurn applies extra payments to your focus debt every payment cycle and cascades freed minimums onto the next debt. Some calculators don't model the cascade or assume different compounding intervals — small differences add up over years.
